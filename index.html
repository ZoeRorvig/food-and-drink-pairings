Instructions

  • Click "Pair Me!" to get a random drink/food combination.
  • To change the food while keeping your drink option, click "Search" under "Food Lookup."
  • To change the drink while keeping your drink option, click "Search" under "Drink Lookup."
  • To filter the food or drink by certain ingredients, enter your Ingredient before clicking "Search."
  • To add a drink/food item to favorites, click "Add to Favorites" on their respective card.
  • To clear favorites and reset the page, click "Clear."
